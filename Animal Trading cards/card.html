Dog

Dogs are descendants of wolves and were the first animals to be domesticated by humans

  • Scientific Name:Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length:70 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan:12 years
  • Habitat:Urban and Rural settings

Dogs, known scientifically as Canis lupus familiaris, are domesticated mammals and beloved companions to humans. Descendants of wolves, dogs have been bred over thousands of years for various traits, leading to a wide range of breeds with different sizes, shapes, and temperaments.
